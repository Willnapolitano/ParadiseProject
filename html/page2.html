Paradise is the name of the device and the location
where your dream is rendered! The name paradise
was chosen precisely because the place is like a real
paradise, there you can travel the world, live in the
world of minecraft if you want! You can participate in
races or travel to space, you can go to other worlds, if
have fun with whoever you want, having the feeling of
being next to each other in person when in real life they
are thousands of kilometers away.


In paradise the map is rendered directly by your brain which avoids lags and crashes, your
only limitation is literally your power to imagine! the difference with paradise is that the
more people connected, the more processing power it has! paradise will use all the dream-generating power of all logged in people to generate fully procedural worlds.
